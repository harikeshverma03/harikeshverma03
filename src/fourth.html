Fourth Generations (1972-Present):Microprocessors

  • The microprocessor brought the fourth generation of computers as thousands of integrated circuits were built onto a single silicon chip. What in the first generation filled an entire room could now fit in the palm of hand.
  • Fourth-generation computers also saw the development of GUI, the mouse and handheld devices.
  • The intel 4004 chip, developed in 1971, located all the components of the computer-from the central processing unit and memory to input/ output controls-on a single chip
